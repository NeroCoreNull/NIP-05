Privacy Facts

  • Privacy is your right to control your personal information and how it's used.
  • Over 80% of Americans feel they have little or no control over the data collected on them by companies.
  • Privacy laws vary significantly around the world, affecting how data is handled across borders.

Free Speech Facts

  • Free speech is considered a fundamental human right as per Universal Declaration of Human Rights.
  • In many countries, the right to free speech is not absolute and comes with responsibilities.
  • Digital platforms have become the new battlegrounds for free speech debates.

Cybersecurity Facts

  • Cybersecurity measures are crucial for protecting data from unauthorized access and cyber threats.
  • The cost of cybercrime to companies worldwide is estimated to reach $10.5 trillion annually by 2025.
  • Human error is the leading cause of cybersecurity breaches.

Data Ethics Facts

  • Data ethics covers fair and responsible use of data, considering the rights and privacy of individuals.
  • Transparency in how organizations collect and use data is crucial for ethical data practices.
  • Ethical data usage requires regular audits and compliance checks to ensure adherence to laws and policies.
