FAQs

What did the Edward Snowden leaks reveal about privacy?

Edward Snowden's 2013 leaks revealed extensive global surveillance programs, many run by the NSA and the Five Eyes Intelligence Alliance with the cooperation of telecommunication companies and European governments, raising significant concerns about privacy.

How can I protect my online privacy?

To protect your online privacy, consider using VPNs, secure email services, and encrypted messaging apps. Regularly update your privacy settings on social media and be cautious about the information you share online.

What are the essentials of internet security?

Essentials of internet security include using strong, unique passwords for different sites, enabling two-factor authentication, keeping software updated, and being aware of phishing schemes and malware.

Why is freedom of speech important in the digital age?

Freedom of speech is crucial for democracy as it fosters an informed and engaged public. In the digital age, this freedom is under threat from censorship, surveillance, and the manipulation of information, highlighting the need for vigilance and protection.
